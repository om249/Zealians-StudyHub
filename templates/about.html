About Zealians StudyHub

Zealians StudyHub is a web-based educational resource platform designed to provide students and faculty with a centralized system for managing academic content such as notes, video lectures, and MCQ tests.

The platform primarily supports MCA, MBA, and IMCA courses and helps streamline digital learning by organizing study material subject-wise and chapter-wise.

Key Features

  • Role-based access for Admin and Students
  • Course, subject, and chapter-wise content organization
  • Secure login with email verification and approval system
  • Online MCQ tests with result tracking
  • Centralized and structured learning resources

Objective

The objective of Zealians StudyHub is to enhance the academic learning experience by providing a reliable digital platform that reduces manual work, improves accessibility, and ensures structured content delivery.
